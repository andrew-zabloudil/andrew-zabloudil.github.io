BoogaBot
A Discord Bot programmed in Python using the Discord.py package for the Discord API. Has moderation commands such as Kick, Ban, and Assign/Remove Role; and general commands that include scraping the web for news about COVID-19 and more.

Pathfinder
A pathfinding visualizer created with Pygame that utilizes Dijkstra's algorithm to find the shortest route between two points placed by the user. The user can also place walls as obstacles or generate a random maze for the algorithm to clear.

ABOUT ME

My first experience with programming was in middle school when I taught myself HTML from a book I begged my parents to buy for me, and I was instantly hooked. In high school I took my school's only two programming courses, but felt lost after completing them. I eventually went to college and, chasing after a desire to have a career with lots of problem solving, obtained a degree in Mechanical Engineering. Even throughout college, though, my love for programming surfaced in a select few classes which used an Arduino. I began learning Japanese on my own during college and also found myself enamored with language learning. After graduating with my degree in Mechanical Engineering, my desire to be a problem solver and love of languages led me to rediscover programming again. With the realization that programming perfectly combines the things I loved about engineering and language learning, I picked up Python and haven't looked back since.
