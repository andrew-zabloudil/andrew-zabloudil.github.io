My Story

My first experience with programming was in middle school when I taught myself HTML from a book I begged my parents to buy for me, and I was instantly hooked. In high school I took my school's only two programming courses, but felt lost after completing them and took a break from programming.

I eventually went to college and, chasing after a desire to have a career with lots of problem solving, obtained a degree in Mechanical Engineering. Even throughout college, though, my love for programming surfaced in a select few classes which used an Arduino. I began learning Japanese on my own during college and also found myself enamored with language learning.

After graduating with my degree in Mechanical Engineering, my desire to be a problem solver and love of languages eventually led me to rediscover programming again. With the realization that programming perfectly combines the things I loved about engineering and language learning, I picked up Python and haven't looked back since.
